Let's Talk

Not Just to create a Store but a vibe For the Visitors.

Celereate is a high-profile store in the United States, and the client was more concerned with the best website design and a good sales marketing campaign to increase sales.
Webreate assisted Celereate, an e-commerce website, in developing a stunningly designed brand and image using multiple templates and page layouts. We helped the company with header tag optimization and internal linking. All the products and their names were optimized using SEO best practices after keyword research. 
We felt it was critical for a company like Celereate, an e-commerce website, to have as many online footprints as possible because its primary business is based on online sales. The goal was to create a unified interactive website that could open interactive product pages to provide end users with all relevant information about their purchases.

PROBLEM

Creating an e-commerce website is always a difficult task. The website had to be designed with the US customer in mind. The website was quite heavy, and it needed to be user-friendly while also being created in a short time.

The company needed to create a unique and seamless website to showcase its products.The website was large and took a long time to load. It was critical to creating a seamless digital environment.

The main issue was categorizing the products because the client specifically targeted the US holiday season. As a result, the pages and clothes should be classified appropriately. It wasn't easy to categorize and develop a conversion rate optimization strategy for a competitive US market.

PROBLEM2

The value of e-commerce sales is expected to reach $7.4 trillion by 2025, with the United States leading the way. It was challenging to develop a strategy for such a competitive market.

• Most visitors are mobile users, and creating such a big website for mobile was difficult. The website must be responsive to mobile devices, also.

• It was challenging to keep the website from bombarding users' screens with unnecessary pop-ups and notifications so that they could enjoy a seamless experience.

• Adding a high-quality product image was critical, and adding genuine and authentic information was difficult.

• Including a smooth and trustworthy payment process is an essential step for any e-commerce website.

• Celereate needed to increase its sales and conversion rate. Creating a well-crafted SEO strategy that could lead to conversion was critical.

PROBLEM3

There will always be issues when creating a website, but we must overcome them to provide the best solution to the customer. Used WooCommerce capabilities to the fullest to create a fully functional online shopping website for selling clothes and other props.

We built an eCommerce website on the WordPress platform, which has a very user-friendly backend for making website updates. We created multiple webpage templates to ensure the content was consistent with the most recent web development and design best practices. All product details were integrated while keeping Google's latest SEO practices in mind.

We linked the website to Google Analytics conversions and goals, allowing clients to optimize their ongoing ad spending. Retargeting was also set up to send messages to customers who abandoned their shopping carts without making a purchase.

PROBLEM4

• We have optimized the website to target US-based users, resulting in a 20% increase in online sales rather than the client's 10% sales requirement.

• We Trained team members for working on the Celereate website on how to work with the WooCommerce theme and update products accordingly.

• The time spent on the online store also increased by 12%.

• The most important aspect was that we could reduce the website's bounce rate to 40%, which was the most significant achievement.
